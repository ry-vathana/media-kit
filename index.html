RY VATHANA

Independent Artist · Southeast Asia

RY VATHANA is an independent music artist known for blending urban street energy with modern hip-hop influences. His music reflects real emotions, confidence, and lifestyle-driven storytelling, creating a sound that connects with both local and international audiences.

Emerging from Southeast Asia, RY VATHANA has built a growing reputation through original releases such as “SUGAR MOMMY,” “WENDY CUT,” and “MOLIKA.” Each track showcases his ability to switch between hard-hitting rhythms, melodic flows, and catchy hooks, while maintaining a strong personal identity.
